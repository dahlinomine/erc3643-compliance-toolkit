# MiCA Articles 16–21 — Issuer Disclosure Checklist
*For asset-referenced token (ART) issuers operating in the EEA*

---

## Art. 17 — White Paper Requirements

- [ ] White paper drafted and reviewed by legal counsel
- [ ] White paper filed with competent national authority (CNA)
- [ ] White paper published on issuer website prior to offering
- [ ] White paper includes all required disclosures:
  - [ ] Description of the ART and its stabilization mechanism
  - [ ] Reserve asset composition and custody arrangements
  - [ ] Rights of holders (including redemption rights)
  - [ ] Risks: market, liquidity, technology, regulatory
  - [ ] Issuer identity, governance, and conflict of interest disclosures
  - [ ] Token issuance and distribution methodology

## Art. 19 — Own Funds Requirement

- [ ] Own funds calculated: minimum of:
  - 350,000 EUR, OR
  - 2% of average amount of reserve assets, OR
  - 1/4 of fixed overhead requirement of preceding year
- [ ] Own funds held in one or more of permitted forms
- [ ] Ongoing monitoring procedure in place

## Art. 20 — Reserve Assets

- [ ] Reserve asset composition documented
- [ ] Reserve assets held in custody with CRD/authorised custodian
- [ ] Custody agreement in place with segregation provisions
- [ ] Reserve assets invested only in permitted instruments (Art. 20(5))
- [ ] Daily reconciliation process established

## Art. 21 — Redemption Rights

- [ ] Redemption policy documented and disclosed in white paper
- [ ] Redemption at par value guaranteed
- [ ] Redemption procedure: `[describe process]`
- [ ] Redemption fees (if any): disclosed in white paper
- [ ] Emergency redemption procedure defined
